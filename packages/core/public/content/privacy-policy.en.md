## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
Our application is designed as a client-side application that stores your data locally in your browser's storage mechanisms, including:
- **LocalStorage:** For persistent application data
- **SessionStorage:** For temporary session data
- **IndexedDB:** For complex data structures (if applicable)

### 4.2 Data Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Data encryption in transit and at rest where applicable
- Secure coding practices and regular security audits
- Protection against common web vulnerabilities (XSS, CSRF, etc.)
- Regular security updates and patches

**Access Controls:**
- Limited access to user data (since data is stored locally)
- Employee training on data protection practices
- Regular review of access permissions

### 4.3 Data Retention
Since data is stored locally on your device:
- Data persistence depends on your browser settings
- Clearing browser data will permanently delete your information
- We do not maintain backups of your local data
- Data retention is entirely under your control
